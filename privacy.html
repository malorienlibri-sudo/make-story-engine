Privacy Policy

Make Story Engine is a private automation application used by its owner to manage and publish digital content, including stories, images, and videos.

The application does not collect, sell, or share personal data with third parties. It only accesses data explicitly authorized by the user through Google services such as Google Drive and Google Sheets in order to automate content creation and publishing workflows.

Data accessed may include files stored in Google Drive and content stored in Google Sheets. This data is used solely for content management and automation.

No data is used for advertising, tracking, or profiling. All data remains the property of the user.
